## The Standard Model Particles

The Standard Model contains **17 fundamental particles** organized by their properties.

## Fermions (Spin 1/2)

Fermions are matter particles obeying **Fermi-Dirac statistics** and the **Pauli exclusion principle**.

### Quarks

| Generation | Up-type | Down-type |
|------------|---------|-----------|
| 1st | up (<span class="math-inline">u</span>) | down (<span class="math-inline">d</span>) |
| 2nd | charm (<span class="math-inline">c</span>) | strange (<span class="math-inline">s</span>) |
| 3rd | top (<span class="math-inline">t</span>) | bottom (<span class="math-inline">b</span>) |

Properties:
- Carry **color charge** (strong interaction)
- Carry **electric charge** (+2/3 or -1/3)
- Never observed in isolation (confinement)

### Leptons

| Generation | Charged | Neutrino |
|------------|---------|----------|
| 1st | electron (<span class="math-inline">e^-</span>) | <span class="math-inline">\nu_e</span> |
| 2nd | muon (<span class="math-inline">\mu^-</span>) | <span class="math-inline">\nu_\mu</span> |
| 3rd | tau (<span class="math-inline">\tau^-</span>) | <span class="math-inline">\nu_\tau</span> |

Properties:
- No color charge (no strong interaction)
- Neutrinos have very small mass

## Bosons (Integer Spin)

Bosons are force carriers obeying **Bose-Einstein statistics**.

### Gauge Bosons (Spin 1)

| Boson | Force | Couples to |
|-------|-------|------------|
| Photon (<span class="math-inline">\gamma</span>) | Electromagnetic | Electric charge |
| Gluon (<span class="math-inline">g</span>) | Strong | Color charge |
| <span class="math-inline">W^\pm</span> | Weak | Weak isospin |
| <span class="math-inline">Z^0</span> | Weak | Weak isospin |

### Higgs Boson (Spin 0)

- Gives mass to <span class="math-inline">W^\pm</span>, <span class="math-inline">Z^0</span>, and fermions
- Discovered at CERN in 2012
- Only fundamental scalar in the Standard Model

## Mathematical Representations

### Gauge Group Structure

The Standard Model gauge group is:

<span class="math-display">G_{SM} = SU(3)_C \times SU(2)_L \times U(1)_Y</span>

| Subgroup | Force | Bosons |
|----------|-------|--------|
| <span class="math-inline">SU(3)_C</span> | Strong | 8 gluons |
| <span class="math-inline">SU(2)_L</span> | Weak | <span class="math-inline">W^1, W^2, W^3</span> |
| <span class="math-inline">U(1)_Y</span> | Hypercharge | <span class="math-inline">B</span> |

After electroweak symmetry breaking:
- <span class="math-inline">W^{1,2} \to W^\pm</span>
- <span class="math-inline">W^3, B \to Z^0, \gamma</span>

### Representations of Fermions

Fermions transform under specific representations:

| Particle | <span class="math-inline">SU(3)_C</span> | <span class="math-inline">SU(2)_L</span> | <span class="math-inline">U(1)_Y</span> |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|
| Left quark doublet | **3** | **2** | +1/6 |
| Right up quark | **3** | **1** | +2/3 |
| Right down quark | **3** | **1** | -1/3 |
| Left lepton doublet | **1** | **2** | -1/2 |
| Right electron | **1** | **1** | -1 |

## Beyond the Standard Model

Particles not in the Standard Model but theorized:
- **Graviton**: Spin-2 carrier of gravity
- **Dark matter candidates**: WIMPs, axions
- **Supersymmetric partners**: Squarks, sleptons, gauginos
